RailGuard Digital Memo System
"From Paper Tracks to Digital Rails."

An elegant, failsafe end-to-end framework designed to digitize physical Guard's Memo Books (OPT-7) for Train Managers across Indian Railways. Eliminating systemic errors, environmental waste, and security risks by deploying responsive, location-verified mobile modules connected to a central control command center.

Pain Points of current operations

Systemic Friction of Legacy Paper Logs

The manual generation, verification, and storage of Guard Memos results in a cascade of structural, financial, and temporal operational friction across Indian Railways.

High Recurring Costs

Continuous procurement of high-grade paper logbooks, specialized binding, and logistical distribution across hundreds of divisions costs Indian Railways crores annually in physical administrative overhead.

Heavy Environmental Footprint

Thousands of manual memo books are completed, collected, and archived daily. This massive volume of paper consumption significantly increases the carbon footprint of railway record management.

Illegibility & Manual Error

Handwritten logs written during transit suffer from illegible penmanship, incorrect formatting, transposition errors in loco pilot names, or missing arrival/departure timestamp entries.

Integrated Platform Topology

RGDMS System Architecture

CLIENT

Flutter Mobile App

Cross-platform app targeting secure Android/iOS devices for Train Managers, offering fast form rendering and local storage capability.

GATEWAY

Node.js API Endpoint

An Express server framework running REST endpoints, handling payload validation, and communicating directly with FOIS databases.

STORAGE

PostgreSQL / PostGIS

A relational dataset structure mapping spatial location indexes, permitting geographic station distance queries for automatic arrivals.

SECURE CLOUD

NIC Gov Cloud Hosting

Deployment on National Informatics Centre secure clouds, adhering to Government of India data privacy guidelines and standards.
